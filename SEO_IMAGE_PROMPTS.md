## Image Specifications for SEO

### File Naming Convention:
- `hero-swiss-residency-main.jpg`
- `hero-swiss-professionals-zurich.jpg`
- `hero-swiss-lifestyle-family.jpg`
- `hero-swiss-background-pattern.jpg`

### Alt Text Recommendations:

1. **Main Hero:**
   "Diverse international family celebrating Swiss residency success in front of Swiss Alps and Lake Geneva, representing successful immigration journey"

2. **Professional Hero:**
   "International professionals working in modern Swiss office in Zurich with Swiss Alps view, representing career opportunities in Switzerland"

3. **Lifestyle Hero:**
   "Happy international family enjoying Swiss countryside lifestyle near Lucerne with Swiss Alps background, representing quality of life in Switzerland"

### Technical Requirements:
- **Dimensions:** 1920x1080px (Full HD) or 2560x1440px (2K) for hero images
- **Format:** WebP (preferred) or JPEG with 85-90% quality
- **File Size:** Under 300KB for web optimization
- **Color Profile:** sRGB
- **Aspect Ratio:** 16:9 for hero images

### SEO Optimization Tips:
1. Include descriptive filenames with keywords
2. Use descriptive alt text (50-125 characters)
3. Add structured data for images in JSON-LD
4. Ensure images are responsive (srcset)
5. Use lazy loading for images below the fold
6. Include relevant keywords naturally in surrounding content

### Image Context for Better SEO:
Place images with relevant H1, H2 headings that include keywords like:
- "Swiss Residency"
- "Immigration to Switzerland"
- "Swiss Work Permit"
- "Swiss Citizenship"
- "Live in Switzerland"
