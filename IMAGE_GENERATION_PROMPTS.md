## Image Specifications & Best Practices

### Technical Requirements:
- **Format:** WebP (preferred) or JPEG with 85-90% quality
- **Color Profile:** sRGB
- **Resolution:** Minimum 2K (1920px width), ideally 4K+ for hero images
- **Aspect Ratios:**
  - Hero: 4:3 or 16:9 (vertical for split layout)
  - Pathway cards: 4:3 (portrait)
  - Backgrounds: 16:9 (wide format)

### SEO Optimization:
- **File Naming:** Use descriptive names with keywords
  - `hero-swiss-modern-building-zurich.jpg`
  - `pathway-eu-freedom-movement.jpg`
  - `pathway-us-professionals-swiss-office.jpg`
  - `pathway-international-family-success.jpg`
  - `cta-swiss-alps-journey.jpg`

- **Alt Text Examples:**
  - Hero: "Modern Swiss office building with Alps view, representing professional success in Switzerland"
  - EU Pathway: "European and Swiss flags representing freedom of movement for EU/EFTA citizens"
  - US Pathway: "North American professionals working in modern Swiss office with mountain view"
  - International: "Diverse international family enjoying Swiss landscape, representing successful immigration"
  - CTA: "Swiss Alps at sunset, representing the journey to Swiss residency"

### Usage Guidelines:
1. **Hero Images:** Use high-resolution images (4K+) for the large display
2. **Pathway Cards:** Images should work well with dark overlays and text
3. **Background Images:** Use subtle opacity (20-30%) so they don't overwhelm content
4. **Optimization:** Compress images while maintaining quality (aim for <500KB per image)
5. **Responsive:** Ensure images look good on all screen sizes

### Design Integration:
- All images should work with gradient overlays
- Consider color harmony with the blue/purple/indigo theme
- Images should complement the modern, clean design aesthetic
- Ensure sufficient contrast for text overlays on pathway cards
