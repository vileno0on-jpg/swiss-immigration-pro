## Image Specifications & Best Practices

### Technical Requirements:
- **Format:** WebP (preferred) or JPEG with 85-90% quality
- **Color Profile:** sRGB
- **Resolution:** Minimum 2K (1920px width), ideally 4K+ for hero images
- **Aspect Ratios:**
  - Layer-specific citizenship: 4:3 (portrait) or 16:9 (landscape)
  - Consultation images: 16:9 (landscape) or 4:3 (portrait)
  - CTA images: 16:9 (wide format)

### SEO Optimization:
- **File Naming:** Use descriptive names with keywords
  - `citizenship-eu-efta-5-year-pathway.jpg`
  - `citizenship-us-canada-10-year-pathway.jpg`
  - `citizenship-international-10-year-pathway.jpg`
  - `consultation-booking-hero.jpg`
  - `consultation-video-call-remote.jpg`
  - `consultation-success-confidence.jpg`
  - `consultation-booking-cta.jpg`
  - `consultation-expert-team.jpg`

- **Alt Text Examples:**

  - US/Canada Citizenship: "North American professional with Swiss passport, representing the 10-year citizenship journey for US and Canadian citizens"
  - International Citizenship: "Diverse international family celebrating Swiss citizenship achievement, representing the 10-year naturalization pathway"
  - Consultation Hero: "Professional immigration consultant in modern Swiss office, offering expert guidance and personalized consultation"
  - Video Consultation: "Remote video consultation setup with Swiss landscape background, representing accessible online immigration advice"
  - Consultation Success: "Confident professional with Swiss immigration documents, representing successful consultation outcomes"

### Usage Guidelines:
1. **Layer-Specific Images:** Use on respective layer pages (/europeans, /americans, /others) to show citizenship pathways
2. **Consultation Images:** Use on consultation booking page (/consultation) and contact pages
3. **Hero Images:** Use high-resolution images (4K+) for large displays
4. **CTA Images:** Ensure images work well with text overlays and buttons
5. **Optimization:** Compress images while maintaining quality (aim for <500KB per image)
6. **Responsive:** Ensure images look good on all screen sizes

### Design Integration:
- All images should work with gradient overlays
- Consider color harmony with the blue/purple/indigo theme
